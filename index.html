About PS Physio

\

I'm Pauline Steenkamp, a qualified healthcare professional and physiotherapist with over ten years of experience in physiotherapy, healthcare management, and patient-centered rehabilitation.

\

My background spans musculoskeletal, orthopedic, Pilates, and aquatic therapy, alongside leadership roles managing multidisciplinary teams and ensuring the highest standards of care.

\

I am passionate about helping people restore movement, reduce pain, and improve quality of life through evidence-based treatment and personalised rehabilitation plans. Whether working in the clinic, on-site, or through mobile services, my goal is to deliver compassionate, effective care that empowers every patient on their recovery journey.

\

We have a lovely space in Harrington Street with client parking.
